Doctor Queue вҖ” Shows patients that nurses have referred for doctor assessment today.
How it works: Nurse clicks "Refer to Doctor" вҶ’ system checks doctor capacity for today. If doctor has space, patient moves here with an assigned time slot. If doctor is full, patient is automatically rescheduled to the next doctor day with available capacity.
Patient slot selection: For return visits, patients choose their own time slot via WhatsApp. They confirm attendance the day before вҖ” unconfirmed slots are released for others.
Capacity limits: Each doctor session has a maximum number of patients (default 10 elective + 4 same-day reserve). Overflow patients are automatically moved to the next available day.
For doctors: Click "Complete" after seeing a patient to record notes, outcome, and any regimen changes. You can also record vitals and add allergies from the patient lookup.
